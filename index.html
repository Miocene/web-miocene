Hi, I'm Julia Miocene

Hi there! I'm a product designer based in Amsterdam with more than eleven years of experience. I specialize in web design, but I also have experience in mobile design. My approach to design is to keep it simple and user-friendly.

Currently, I'm part of the IDE team at GitLab, where I'm focused on Web IDE and Remote Development.

Previously, I worked at NeuroDub, where I designed all the user interfaces and my first ever branding! I also had the opportunity to work at big companies like Yandex and Kontur.

I love building design systems that can scale. My last open-source design system project called Consta.

I'm also passionate about CSS and enjoy creating CSS Art and Animations. One fun fact about me: I'm a Google Developer Expert on Web!
